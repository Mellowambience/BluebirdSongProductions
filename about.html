About

Bluebird Song Productions is an independent music production label and publishing imprint. We exist to give strange, serious, beautiful work a real home.

We are not a vanity label. We are not a content factory. We are a small, intentional entity built for artists who are building worlds — artists whose work exists at the edge of what categories can contain.

Our catalog spans music, fiction, encoded transmissions, and whatever form comes next. Our standard is simple: does it mean something?
